About syllagent

Who am I?

I'm Daniel, a writer and educator learning about AI, learning design, and teaching.

What is syllagent?

syllagent is my space for writing, research, and reflections on teaching in the age of artificial intelligence. Here, I explore how AI agents and large language models are transforming education and what that means for teachers and learners.

What you'll find here

Essays on AI and pedagogy, research findings, experiments, and reflections. Maybe even some very poor philosophy. My feelings and thinking will change and I hope this site captures that.
